### On UI design — honest advice:

**Tools to use outside of code:**
- **Figma** (free) — industry standard, design screens visually before building them. Huge time saver. You can share designs with anyone for feedback.
- **Mobbin** (mobbin.com) — real screenshots from top apps. Best reference for dark UI, couples apps, travel apps. Use it for inspiration before designing anything.
- **Dribbble** — search "dark mobile app" or "travel app UI" for inspiration
- **Screenshot your own app** — put it next to apps you love (Paired, Spotify, Apple Maps) and compare. What feels different? That gap is your design brief.

**The honest workflow:**
1. Screenshot current Roamie screen
2. Open Figma, paste it
3. Draw over it with what you want it to look like
4. Show me the Figma and I build it

That's faster than describing it in words. One Figma screen = 10 minutes of building vs 30 minutes of back and forth.
